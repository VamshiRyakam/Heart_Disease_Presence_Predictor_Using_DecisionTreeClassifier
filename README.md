# **Heart_Disease_Presence_Predictor_Using_DecisionTreeClassifier**

### Cloumns Description
**age:** Age of the patient.

**sex:** Gender of the Patient (1 = Male, 0 = Female)

**chest pain:** Chest Pain in 4 categories (0,1,2,3)

**restbp:** Resting blood presurre

**cholestrol:** Cholestrol Level of the patient.

**fast blood sugar:** Fasting blood sugar.

**rest ecg:** ECG of the patient. (0 means normal, 1 means problem)

**max heart rate:** Maximum heart rate achieved.

**exercise-included:** Exercise-included angina (1 = yes, 0 = no).

**oldpeak:** ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.

**slope:** Slope of the peak exercise: (0 = Upsloping, 1 = Flat , 2 = Downsloping).

**No. of major vessels:** Number of Major Vessels.

**thalessemia:** Thalessemia

**Heart disease:** 0 = no heart disease , 1= presence of heart disease.
